TradePulse Signal Formula

Long Opportunity & Short Opportunity classification rules

Long Signal Formula

ConditionRequirement
PriceSideways (consolidation) — |move| ≤ 2% across 4h→5m
Open Interest↑↑↑ Strong rise (≥ 3% on 1h/4h)
VolumeSlightly increasing (+0.3% to +5%)
FlowInflow > Outflow (checked 4h → 5m timeframes)
Funding RateNegative or slightly negative
Long LiquidationsLow

Short Signal Formula

ConditionRequirement
PriceBig move OR sideways
Open Interest↑↑↑ Strong rise (≥ 3%)
VolumeSlightly decreasing (−5% to −0.3%)
FlowOutflow > Inflow (checked 4h → 5m)
Funding RateHighly positive (≥ 0.03%)
Long/Short RatioToo many shorts (ratio ≤ 0.92)

Classification

Score each side: 6 conditions per formula.
Long Opportunity: ≥ 5/6 long conditions met (≥ 4/6 with strong OI + funding).
Short Opportunity: ≥ 5/6 short conditions met (≥ 4/6 with strong OI + funding).
Otherwise: Neutral.
